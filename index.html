Food

The roast suckling pig is called Lechón in the Philippines, and Lechón means in Spanish Suckling pig, it is generally believed that during the colonial period hundreds of years ago, the Spanish brought the dish "roast suckling pig" to the Philippines. Because of its convenient processing and delicious taste, it has gradually become a favorite food of the locals and spread it.
